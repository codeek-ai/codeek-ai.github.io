Scanner Utility

QR and Barcode

QR and Barcode is a fast scanner app built for everyday use. It helps users scan codes, understand the content instantly, take the next action quickly, and create their own QR codes.

What It Does

The app keeps scanning simple, fast, and privacy-focused while supporting common real-world workflows such as Wi-Fi joining, contact saving, link opening, and QR creation.

  • Scans QR codes and major barcode formats
  • Detects links, Wi-Fi, contacts, phone, email, SMS, maps, and plain text
  • Lets users open, copy, or share scan results instantly
  • Saves useful scans to local history with search and filters
  • Creates QR codes for common use cases from guided forms

App Details

  • Privacy-first local storage
  • No account required for core features
  • Designed for quick everyday actions
