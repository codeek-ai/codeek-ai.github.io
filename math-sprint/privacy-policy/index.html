Math Sprint Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 4, 2026

App page All policies

Math Sprint is a local, offline-first practice app for math and memory.

Information We Collect

Math Sprint does not collect personal information from users.

Math Sprint does not require account creation and does not ask users to provide name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

Data Stored on the Device

Math Sprint stores limited app data only on the user's device so the app can work offline and show personal progress features.

  • App settings such as language, haptics, sound placeholder preferences, and high contrast mode
  • Game mode and difficulty
  • Score, correct answer count, total question count, elapsed time, and bonus points
  • Date and time of play
  • Daily challenge completion history and streak state
  • Local analytics event logs used for product debugging and future local analysis

This information is stored locally on the device and is not sent to our servers.

Data Sharing

Math Sprint does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

Internet and Network Use

Math Sprint is designed to work without creating user accounts or sending gameplay data to a backend service. At this time, Math Sprint does not transmit personal data to us or to third parties.

Children

Math Sprint is intended for general practice gameplay and may be used by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because gameplay history is stored locally on the device, users can clear app data or uninstall the app to remove that stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted with a new effective date.
