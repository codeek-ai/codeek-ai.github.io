Codeek Camera Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

App page All policies

This Privacy Policy explains how Codeek Camera handles information when you use the mobile application and any public website page where this policy is posted.

Codeek Camera is designed to capture photos with camera access, live location context, and local gallery saving. The app does not require account creation and does not provide an in-app social feed or public profile system.

Information We Access

  • Camera access so you can take photos inside the app
  • Foreground location permission so the app can read current GPS coordinates and attempt to show a human-readable address through reverse geocoding
  • Photos and media library access so the app can save captured photos to your gallery and load saved photos for viewing inside the app gallery

How Information Is Used

  • Show a live camera preview
  • Capture a photo when you choose to take one
  • Read your current location while the app is in use
  • Display location details such as coordinates and address
  • Save captured photos to your device photo library
  • Display saved photo items in the in-app gallery view

Data Storage

  • Photos you capture are saved to your device photo library
  • Location details are used in the active app session for display purposes
  • The app does not currently provide account-based cloud sync or require sign-in

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Codeek Camera does not include an in-app feature that sells your personal information. Sharing may still occur if required by law, to protect rights, safety, and security, or if the app is transferred as part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

Permissions

  • Camera: to capture photos
  • Location while in use: to read live GPS information
  • Photos or media library: to save images and view saved photos

You can deny these permissions, but parts of the app may not work correctly without them.

Data Retention

Photos remain in your photo library until you delete them. App-session metadata may remain only for the duration of app use unless future versions store it more permanently.

Children

Codeek Camera is not intentionally directed to children under 13.

Security

No method of electronic storage, device access, or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, the last updated date should be revised and the updated version should be published with the app release.
