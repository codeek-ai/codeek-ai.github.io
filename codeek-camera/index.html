Codeek Camera

Codeek Camera is a modern GPS camera app for people who need photos with live location context. It is designed for both personal memories and professional proof-of-location workflows.

What It Does

The app focuses on fast camera capture with trusted metadata. It helps users document job sites, deliveries, travel moments, inspections, and field work with clean, proof-ready context.

  • Live camera preview with front and back camera switching
  • GPS coordinates and reverse-geocoded address details
  • Current date and time metadata
  • Save photos to gallery and access them in-app
  • Built to expand into stamped overlays, share flow, and AI captions

App Details
