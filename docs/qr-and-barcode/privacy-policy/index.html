QR and Barcode Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

App page All policies

QR and Barcode respects your privacy. This app is designed to work primarily on your device and does not require you to create an account.

Information We Collect

  • Camera access for scanning QR codes and barcodes
  • Scan content you choose to scan
  • History data saved locally on your device
  • App settings such as theme and preferences

How We Use Information

  • Scanning QR codes and barcodes
  • Showing scan results
  • Saving scan history on your device
  • Remembering your app preferences

Data Storage

  • Scan history is stored locally on your device
  • App preferences are stored locally on your device
  • No account registration is required
  • The app does not intentionally upload scan history to our servers

Permissions

The app requests camera permission so you can scan QR codes and barcodes.

Sharing

If you choose to use share features, the content you share is sent only through the system sharing options you select.

Third-Party Services

The current version of the app does not require sign-in or cloud sync. If analytics, crash reporting, or other third-party services are added in a future release, this Privacy Policy should be updated before release.

Android 13 and Advertising ID

The current version of QR and Barcode does not use the Android advertising ID for ads or profiling and does not require advertising features to use the core scanning experience.

Data Retention

Your scan history remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed specifically to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a backend service.

Security

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • Deny camera permission
  • Delete saved history from the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data, subject to device behavior

Contact

Contact email: enesk6u@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this document.
